How to Sign up for MyChart

(Printable | Video)

Starting on February 25, 2025, when you log into MyChart, you will be prompted to set up Two-Step Verification. Two-Step Verification is an extra layer of security that sends a code to your mobile phone or email, during MyChart login, to confirm your identity. For more information about Two-Step Verification, click here.

  • Pay your Bill Online

    Sign in to request an estimate, view billing details, make online payments, and set up a payment plan. You can also ask billing questions by sending a MyChart message to customer service. You can also pay as a guest.

  • Update Your Personal Information

    Provide updated insurance and demographic information including race, ethnicity, language, and identity.

  • Schedule & Manage Appointments

    Schedule and request appointments. You can also view past appointments, reschedule or cancel appointments, and register before you arrive.

  • View Results & Records

    View your health history and get lab test results as soon as they are available.

  • Access Someone Else’s MyChart

    Proxy access in MyChart allows you to stay connected and manage someone else’s healthcare. Adult patients can invite someone to access their record through the Sharing Hub, found under the main menu. For patients under 18, access can be requested in person at your provider's office or through the online Proxy Access Form.

  • Download the MyChart Mobile App

    Take MyChart on the go and stay connected to your health information no matter where you are with the MyChart mobile app. Visit the App Store or the Google Play Store to download the app today!
