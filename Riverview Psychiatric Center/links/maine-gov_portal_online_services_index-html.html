Online Services

Maine.gov online services make it possible to complete your transactions with state and local government online, saving you time and hassle.

Online services include interactive features such as payments, registrations, licensing and permit applications, purchases, searches, email notifications, and more.

Learn more about online service fees, privacy, security, and help. Some online services are available only to InforME subscribers. If you are a frequent user of online business services you may want to explore becoming an InforME subscriber.

Featured Online Services

Absentee Ballot Request

Use the online service to submit an electronic request for an absentee ballot from your municipality, or create a ready-to-mail printable form.

Alcohol Seller and Server Training

This online service provides a complete training course for sellers and servers of alcoholic beverages within the State of Maine. After reviewing the training material, users can take the online assessment to obtain certification. Upon successful completion of the assessment, you will receive a printable certificate.

Campground Reservations

Check campsite availability and reserve your campsite in Maine State Parks. You can also purchase a state park pass.

Corporate Fraud Monitoring

Allows users to monitor one or more business entities for fraudulent filings.

Corporations Search

Search for a corporation registered with the Office of the Secretary of State.

Court Fines & Fees Payment Service

Pay court fines and fees online with a credit card.

Driver's License/ID Renewal & Replacement

Renew or replace your digital driver's license or Maine ID card.

Health Inspection Program License Renewals

This online service allows for the renewal of licenses under the Division of Environmental Health, Health Inspection Program.

Hunting & Fishing Licenses

Purchase hunting and fishing licenses online.

Maine JobLink

Maine's online resource for job seekers.

Maine Open Checkbook

Moose Permit Winners Bulk Data Sales

My Maine Connection

My Maine Connection is a one-stop connection to the Food Supplement Program, Medical Assistance, Temporary Assistance to Needy Families (TANF) and/or Child Care. A screening tool helps you find out if you are eligible. The website also has an online application to apply for these programs.

One Stop Background Check

A complete and official state background check is now available in a single, combined report. This online service allows you to order a combined report on an individual in one easy transaction, including checks of several state sources. Using this service ensures the data you receive will be accurate and up-to-date, as it comes directly from the latest official data of the Maine State Bureau of Identification and the Maine Bureau of Motor Vehicles.
