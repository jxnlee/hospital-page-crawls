About us

St. Joseph Healthcare, a member of Covenant Health, was founded in 1947 by the Felician Sisters with a mission to support the overall wellness and healing of a patient’s mind, body and spirit. St. Joseph Hospital, located in Bangor, Maine, is its 112-bed acute care community hospital. It is modern, accredited and offers medical, surgical, orthopedic and cardiopulmonary care, laboratory services, physical and occupational therapy as well as a 24-hour Emergency Department and a Skin and Wound Healing Center. OUR VALUES Compassion We show respect, caring and sensitivity towards all, honoring the dignity of each person, especially the poor, vulnerable and suffering. Integrity We promote justice and ethical behavior, and responsibly steward our human, financial and environmental resources. Collaboration We work in partnership, dialogue and shared purpose to create healthy communities. Excellence We deliver all services with he highest level of quality, while seeking creative innovation. Serving Patients and Community St. Joseph Healthcare employs more than 800 people in more than 30 departments. Our dedicated people extend the very best care to others by living our wholistic mission each and every day...a mission that encompasses compassion, competence and community. To learn more about us, please visit www.stjoeshealing.org.

    Congratulations Donna Voelker, CNA, our first 2025 recipient of the Sunshine Award at St. Joseph Healthcare! The Sunshine Award recognizes Certified Nursing Assistants (CNAs) who brighten the days of their patients, families, and co-workers. Honorees are selected based on demonstration of the St. Joseph Hospital Values of Compassion, Integrity, Collaboration, and Excellence. Donna was nominated by a patient who shared: “I was recently admitted to the hospital for the first time, having never been hospitalized before except to give birth, and I was very apprehensive. There’s no way to fully express how much I appreciated the love and care I received from exceptional people. I can’t name them all, but they are in my heart—especially my CNA, Donna. Donna’s sense of humor matched my own, which made me feel understood and at ease. She constantly reassured me, kept me comfortable, and always respected my dignity. Every time she entered my room, she brought warmth and light with her calm, professional demeanor. Despite how busy she was, she always made time for me, and her presence lifted my spirits. Donna is 100% deserving of the Sunshine Award. Thank you, Donna!” If you want to nominate a St. Joe's CNA for The Sunshine Award, fill out a nomination form in person, by mail, or online.
