Our Journey to Excellence

Positioned to Grow. Together.

At Covenant Health, our strategy is clear. In the simplest terms, our Catholic identity serves as our foundation. Our patients and residents at the apex of all we do. To ensure we meet their needs and our goals, we focus on our Mission, Values and Vision. Our strategic plan is our compass and positions us to grow our ministry of healing and health.
