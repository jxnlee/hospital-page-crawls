Surgical Services

Northern Light Mercy Hospital is nationally recognized for excellence in surgical outcomes, safety, and patient experience, and we are known throughout Maine for the personalized and compassionate care we provide.

Mercy’s surgical practices offer a wide range of services, including spine surgery, orthopedics, breast care, women's health, general surgery, and ear, nose, and throat care. When you become our patient, your care team will support and guide you throughout the process – from consultation to surgery, successful discharge, and home care. We invite you to view a patient experience video to learn more about a typical surgical patient’s journey at Mercy Hospital.

Partnering in the care of our community

Mercy Hospital partners with community physicians and practices that perform surgical procedures in our facilities, including those that provide the following services:

Urology

Benign prostate hyperplasia (BPH), all urologic cancers (prostate, bladder, kidney, testicular, ureteral, adrenal), kidney stones, urinary incontinence, overactive bladder (OAB), erectile dysfunction (ED), infertility/low sperm counts, hydrocele, varicocele, circumcision, robotic surgery, laparoscopic surgery, Aquablation, urinary tract infection (UTI), blood in urine (hematuria), urinary retention, bladder prolapse, uterus prolapse, rectocele, pelvic pain, and recurrent urinary tract infections.

Endovascular AAA repair, open and endovascular treatment for peripheral arterial disease, dialysis access and maintenance, endovascular deep venous therapy, catheter based varicose vein treatment, minimally invasive therapy for vascular malformations.
