Portland's Orthopedic and Sports Medicine Experts

Now offering same day access for urgent needs: 207-781-4002

We offer Portland, South Portland, Falmouth, and surrounding communities same day access for urgent orthopedic needs and sports related injuries. We are committed to providing quality, personalized, and compassionate orthopedic services to the communities we serve.

We treat adult patients suffering from acute sports injury or chronic pain of the knee, hip, and shoulder

  • Surgical consultations for hip pain, knee pain, shoulder pain, sports injuries, and chronic conditions 
  • Minimally invasive surgical techniques
  • Shoulder arthroscopy for rotator cuff, labral tear, and dislocation repair  
  • Hip arthroscopy for impingement, labral tears, and chronic pain
  • Meniscal debridement of the knee and repair
  • Ligament and tendon repair of the knee
  • Injections for pain to restore function and activity
  • Sports medicine: Click here to watch a WMTW-TV interview on how to avoid winter sports injuries with sports medicine expert Dr. Benjamin Huffard.

Leaders in sports injury surgery and hip, knee, and shoulder replacement

The joint replacement experts at Northern Light Mercy Orthopedics specialize in using minimally invasive techniques for:
  • Total hip, total knee, and partial knee replacement surgery
  • Total shoulder replacement and reverse shoulder replacement approaches


At Northern Light Mercy Orthopedics, we take a team approach, customizing a care plan that meets the individual needs of each patient. Our team will be with you every step of the way to answer questions and provide the support you need to get back to enjoying life. If you have any questions or want to learn more about our practice and referral process, please fill out the form below or give us a call at 207-781-4002.
